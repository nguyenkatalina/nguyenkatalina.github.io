Pieces By The Hand

An Art Kiosk Made For Handbuilt Pottery Pieces

CHALLENGE: The design goal was to create a kiosk/booth that does not exceed a 10ft x 10ft x 10ft box.
SOLUTION: With this being one of the first projects I've done with using SketchUp, I wanted to create a space that would be compact, but could also expand into a bigger area for different things, whether that means for display or for purchasing items.
