MENTAL CAPACITY

ˈmen-tᵊl kə-ˈpa-sə-tē
A compilation of gifs that represent the chaos going through my mind as I go through finals season.


I used images from throughout the semester to dial everything in to the last few weeks of the semester, and then pixelated those images to be a representation of my brain cells. From there, I glitched those pixelated images to represent my brain feeling overwhelmed and overstimulated. Using those images, I created 10 gifs from each image which visualizes my brain and the millions of things going on in my head.
