Fabric Prints

Visual Manipulation


The design goal was to create fabric patterns using illustrator and photoshop to draw and trace objects (one being a body part and the other two being random objects. By manipulating the size, scale, color, line weights, opacity, etc., I was able to design three different patterns with the same three objects. For this project, the objects I used were my hand, a maskk, and a mechanical keyboard switch.
