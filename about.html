A little about me!


I'M KATALINA NGUYEN!

I'm currently a student studying interior design. This wasn't always my plan as I was originally a business major who switched to mechanical engineering. Interior design was a career choice always at the back of my mind, but never came to light because of the unsure financial security that would come with it. I eventually came to the realization that I wasn't happy with where I was in my life and decided to make the full transition into focusing on a career in interior design. The field is still so broad and new to me, but that is the fun of having such a career.

WHAT DO I BELIEVE IN?

I strive to be able to create spaces where the memories are made to last. These memories will live beyond that of the space itself so that people can remember pieces of their lives even when a space has changed or is no longer there. Whether simple with minimal furniture or complex with many colors, I want these spaces to invoke a feeling of comfort as people begin to experience a space. I believe in functionality in the things and spaces I create and work in. This being said I want to design things meant to be used daily with practicality in mind. My designs will involve creating a space for people to show their personalities while considering comfort and functionality.
