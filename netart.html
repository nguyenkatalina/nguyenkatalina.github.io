Stop And Take A Break

The challenge was to create and connect a series of pages around a topic, story, or experience, with using links, images, and pages to tell the story in a modern, hypertext, yet poetic way.My website takes its user and tells them to take a break. In a busy world where we are always doing something and thinking about something, we often need reminders that it's time for a break. This site runs through options you can take for your break, from breathing to taking a walk, to reprioritizing. It's very important to just unclench your jaw once in a while to relax.


Click through the pages for a break away from everything else!
