Boba Game

I created a simple game using P5.js that allows the player to catch falling boba perals.I did a boba game to collect the boba pearl into the cup because of the popularity of boba. The cup on the bottom of the canvas can move left and right using the left and right arrow keys. If the pearl hits the cup, the player's score increases by 1 and the ball resets to the top of the screen at a random location. Every time the player's score reaches a multiple of 5, the level increases by 1 and the ball and player speed up, mimicking the speed at which orders happen during a busy hour. If the ball reaches the bottom of the screen without colliding with the player, the player's score resets to 0.
